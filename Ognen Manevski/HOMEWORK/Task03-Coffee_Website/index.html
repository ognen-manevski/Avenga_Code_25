Welcome to Coffee Corner

Coffee is more than just a drink – it’s a daily ritual, a moment of peace, and a way to connect with people. From strong espresso shots to sweet, creamy lattes, coffee is enjoyed in homes, offices and cafés all around the world.

On this website, we celebrate coffee: how it tastes, how it’s made, and why so many people can’t imagine starting their day without it.

Featured Coffee Types

Here are some of our most popular coffee drinks. Click a coffee to jump straight to it in the store.

  • Espresso

    A strong, concentrated shot of coffee with a rich aroma and intense flavour.

    View Espresso

  • Latte

    Smooth espresso mixed with plenty of steamed milk and a thin layer of foam.

    View Latte

  • Cappuccino

    A classic blend of espresso, steamed milk and milk foam, often topped with cocoa.

    View Cappuccino

  • Cold Brew

    Coffee brewed slowly with cold water for a smooth, less acidic and refreshing taste.

    View Cold Brew

  • Americano

    Espresso diluted with hot water for a smoother but still bold coffee taste.

    View Americano

  • Flat White

    A velvety coffee drink made with espresso and finely textured steamed milk.

    View Flat White

  • Mocha

    A delicious mix of espresso, chocolate syrup and steamed milk.

    View Mocha

  • Macchiato

    Espresso "stained" with a small amount of foamed milk for a strong taste.

    View Macchiato

  • Irish Coffee

    Hot coffee mixed with Irish whiskey, sugar and topped with cold cream.

    View Irish Coffee

  • Affogato

    A dessert-style coffee with a shot of hot espresso poured over vanilla ice cream.

    View Affogato

  • Turkish Coffee

    Finely ground coffee brewed unfiltered in a small pot, rich and intense.

    View Turkish Coffee

  • Ristretto

    A shorter, more concentrated version of espresso with a bold flavour.

    View Ristretto

Coffee Fun Fact

Did you know that coffee was discovered, according to legend, by an Ethiopian goat herder who noticed his goats were full of energy after eating coffee cherries?

Today, people around the world drink millions of cups of coffee every single day!
