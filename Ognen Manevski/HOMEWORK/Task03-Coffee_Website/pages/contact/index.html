Community Coffee Picks

Vanilla Iced Latte

Shared by: Ana

My favourite summer drink! I brew one shot of espresso, pour it over ice, add cold milk and a splash of vanilla syrup. It’s sweet, refreshing, and perfect for hot days.

Best enjoyed: At home

Classic Turkish Coffee

Shared by: Marko

I use very finely ground coffee, cold water, and a little sugar. Everything goes into a cezve and is slowly heated without stirring. When the foam rises, I pour it into the cup. Strong and intense!

Best enjoyed: In a café

Chocolate Mocha

Shared by: Elena

I mix one espresso with hot milk and one spoon of chocolate syrup. Sometimes I add whipped cream on top for an extra treat. It feels like dessert in a cup.

Best enjoyed: At work / school

Honey Cinnamon Cappuccino

Shared by: Stefan

A regular cappuccino with a teaspoon of honey and a light sprinkle of cinnamon. The honey adds natural sweetness and the cinnamon gives it a warm aroma.

Best enjoyed: At home

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you really serve coffee, or is this just a school project?

Coffee Corner is a fictional café created as a web development homework project. All content is for educational purposes only.

Can I order coffee online from this website?

No, you cannot place real orders. The store page is a practice layout that shows example products and prices.

How can I share my coffee recipe with you?

You can use the Coffee Recommendations form on this page to describe your favourite drink and how to prepare it.

What skills were used to build this website?

This website was built using HTML and CSS, with a simple structure and semantic elements such as sections, articles, navigation, and forms.
